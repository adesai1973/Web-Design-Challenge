Wind Speed vs. Latitude

This plot shows Wind Speed of cities versus it's latitudes.

Cities do not show direct correlation to it's latitude. General observation from plot is that most cities are on average less windy (wind speeds less than 10mph). The cities closer to equator seem to have wider data spread on less windy and more windy cities in general. Cities with winds more than 25 mph are typically around 30 degs latitude off the equator.

Weather.govhas a good detailed explanation of winds and factors affecting winds globally.
