Welcome to Hawaii

Hawaii is known for amazing weather. To accomplish this analysis we first pulled data from 9 weather stations and precipitation and temperature measurements for each station - daily for 2010-2017.

After assembling the dataset, we plotted 12 month precipitation and 12 month temperature data. This site provides the datsources (measurements and stations), plots and analysis.

Hawaiian Phrases - it is the Hawaiian spirit...

It is customary to say "Aloha" and "Mahalo" - some key terms visitors should familiarize themselves with. Here is some basic terminology and popular things in Hawaii for visitors:

  • Aloha - Hello or Goodbye
  • Mahalo - Thank you
  • Kamaâ€™aina - Name for local Hawaiian resident regardless of ethnicity or race
  • Ohana - means Family - an extended use of term for including others
  • Lanai - Patio or Balcony
  • Shaka - very popular hand gesture of extended thumb and pinkie. This gesture symbolizes the Aloha spirit or the feeling of friendship, understanding, or solidarity.
